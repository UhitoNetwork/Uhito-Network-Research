# Contributing to Uhito Network Research

## Discussion Welcome, Copying Not
We welcome:
- Constructive feedback in Issues
- Personal insights and experiences
- Questions that deepen understanding

We do NOT accept:
- Pull requests copying content elsewhere
- Requests to remove licensing
- Commercial collaboration offers

## How to Engage Respectfully
1. Open an Issue with your thoughts
2. Stay on topic: hacker psychology and solutions
3. Respect the research labor involved
4. Understand this is view-only research, not open-source

## Violations Will Be Removed
Comments attempting to circumvent licensing or encourage content theft will be deleted.
